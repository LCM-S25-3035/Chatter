Ruhpolding Traunstein district in southeastern Bavaria, Germany. It is situated in the south of the Chiemgau region in the Alps and Municipality next to the Austrian border. The economy is based on tourism and sports.Major winter. sports held in the district include biathlon and Ski jumping. Other sports which are possible for tourists and residents are golf, mountainbiking, shooting, hiking, fly fishing and skiing.
